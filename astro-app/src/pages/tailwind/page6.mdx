**Diseño Responsive y Estados** (mobile-first)  
- **Prefijos de breakpoints**:  
  - `sm:` ≥ 640 px, `md:` ≥ 768 px, `lg:` ≥ 1024 px, `xl:` ≥ 1280 px  
- **Estados**:  
  - `hover:`, `focus:`, `active:`, `dark:`  
- **Ancho dinámico**:  
  - `w-full`, `sm:w-auto`, `md:w-1/2`, `lg:w-1/3`  
- **Tipografía adaptable**:  
  - `text-sm`, `md:text-base`, `lg:text-lg`  

```astro
<button
  class="
    w-full sm:w-auto
    bg-purple-500 hover:bg-purple-800
    text-white
    px-4 py-2 sm:px-6 md:px-8 lg:px-10
    text-sm md:text-base lg:text-lg
    rounded
  "
>
  Botón Responsivo
</button>
```
